About Yogshalaa

Yogshalaa is a Online Yoga Platform especially designed to indulge people around the Globe in practicing Yoga and Meditation- Whether if you are a busy mom looking for a quick Energy flow in the morning, or a beginner to brush up on the Basics of Yoga, or a traveler in a need to relax with a Yog Nidra. Whatever your level of Experience, Aspirations and Life Stage - We are here to create an Online Yoga Program for you.
